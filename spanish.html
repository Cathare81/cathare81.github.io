    Welcome

    Energy is vital for the operability of the factories and service companies around the world. It also provides decent conditions of life for people at home. The mission of ANSAB whole team is to assess, mitigate and communicate risks associated with production, transport/distribution, process/stock of energy based on nuclear power, oil, gas and electricity.

    Features and services

    Designed to be adaptable and proactive, ANSAB risk management aims to address the challenge posed by variability and uncertainty of complex systems.

    • Safety analysis

      Accident analysis for nuclear industry

    • Risk-based inspection analysis

      When, where, and how to inspect equipment of oil and gas industry

    • Energy network analysis

      Secure energy supply, assess the level of independence of an electric network

    • Organizational intelligence

      Analysis of organizational factors which impact on human reliability

    • Risk communication assessment

      How to communicate about risk inside and outside your organization
